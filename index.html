Personal Statement

I am Mulder, a Maine Coon cat who thinks he's a dog. I have many skills and attributes to offer the business world including problem solving, creative thinking and negotiation. I am an excellent team player but also work well independently.

Skills and Attributes

Problem Solving

Overcoming my lack of opposable thumbs, I have trained myself to open cupboards and fridges, turn the kitchen tap on and many other useful tasks. Once I have learnt something I tend to retain the knowledge forever. I am also ruthlessly efficient in what I learn, for example I have never seen the benefit of learning how to turn the tap off again.

Teamwork

  • I have worked well with colleagues to achieve things my employers never thought possible, like getting into the kitchen bin to eat eggshells or opening a sealed cupboard and chewing through the big food bags.
  • I have been a mentor to new members of the team, and successfully built relationships with colleagues even though they stole my favourite sleeping spots.
  • I am friendly and approachable and always supportive of colleagues, will never hiss or scratch.

Negotiation Skills

  • Succesfully negotiated for meal times to be earlier on a regular basis since starting in my role.
  • Increased number of treats by trading them for cute tricks.

Communication Skills

  • I am vocal and articulate, known as a good communicator with both colleagues and my employer
